# Tulpar Auto Competitor SEO Notes

## Reviewed Sites

- https://luxcarastana.kz/
- https://astana-express.kz/
- https://arsrental.kz/

## What Competitors Do Well

### LuxCarAstana

- Uses a direct commercial H1 around premium car rental in Astana.
- Shows many vehicle categories: premium, business, SUVs, limousines, minibuses and buses.
- Builds internal links for extra services: airport transfer, car with driver, delegations, weddings, photoshoots, minivans, hourly rental and intercity routes.
- Has long FAQ and SEO text near the bottom of the page.
- Shows social proof and 2GIS-style reviews.

### Astana Express

- Has a clear service taxonomy: intercity, tourism, corporate, children transport, transfer, wedding convoy.
- Strong in bus/minibus and group transportation intent.
- Uses calculator and tariff-style language, which is useful for conversion.
- Repeats service links in footer for crawlability.

### ARS Rental

- Targets "аренда авто с водителем в Алматы и Астане" directly.
- Uses luxury fleet names as search signals: Rolls-Royce, Mercedes-Benz W223, W222, V-Class, Sprinter VIP, Maybach.
- Breaks rental intent into delegation, photoshoot, excursion, event, wedding, daily rental, transfer, intercity with driver, personal driver and security.
- Has gallery, reviews, fleet and service pages.

## Tulpar Auto Positioning

Tulpar Auto should not copy the competitors' broad "everything rental" tone. The stronger position is:

- premium chauffeur service for business and VIP clients;
- airport transfer and meet-and-greet;
- corporate transport with documents and coordination;
- delegation and event transport;
- three-city coverage: Astana, Almaty, Shymkent;
- three-language coverage: Russian, English, Chinese.

## Implemented In Project

- Language/city/service route structure.
- Dynamic `title`, `description`, canonical, `hreflang`, JSON-LD and FAQ schema.
- Search-friendly service slugs for English and Chinese.
- City-specific demand blocks.
- Service-specific use cases, inclusions and FAQ.
- `sitemap.xml`, `robots.txt` and SPA redirect fallback.

## Next SEO Improvements

- Add real separate vehicle detail pages for each car model.
- Add pricing or "minimum order" blocks if the business is ready to publish conditions.
- Add review schema only after real verified reviews are added.
- Add static prerendering or SSR so every URL has full HTML content before JavaScript runs.
